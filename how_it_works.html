How It Works

Skill Growth & Collaboration

Growing our skills can be done on our own, but together we can go beyond our limits. This platform allows users to find other group members to collaborate on projects and sharpen their skills regardless of experience.

Knowledge and Practice

For programmers across all levels, a Learning Center would host links to tutorials and other resources needed to help begin learning the foundations of information technology.

Support throughout Development

Along with collaboration, we hope to foster a network and supportive community for skill building. The tools brought together would support students from non-traditional backgrounds to those with years of experience.

DevConnect, uplifting together for the stars and beyond
